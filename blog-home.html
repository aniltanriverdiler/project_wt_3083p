Spectra + Partners opens new office in Poland

Spectra + Partners has opened a new office on the 44th floor of the Varso Tower, the tallest building in the EU, located in Warsaw. The office, with stunning views of the city, houses 30 employees and supports key projects like the CPK airport development. The studio reflects the practice’s commitment to Warsaw and Poland’s importance within Europe. Designed with spacious open-plan offices and social spaces, Varso Tower exemplifies the firm's design ethos.

Construction gathers momentum at Techo International Airport in Cambodia

Spectra + Partners is leading the construction of Techo International Airport in Phnom Penh, Cambodia, a modern, sustainable gateway inspired by Cambodian culture. The terminal features a central head house and aerofoil-shaped piers, combining efficient passenger flow with lush greenery and energy from an onsite photovoltaic farm. Designed for phased expansion, the airport will initially handle 13 million passengers annually, with future phases accommodating up to 30 million.
