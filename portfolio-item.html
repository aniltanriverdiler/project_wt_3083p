1 Nanjing Road

1 Nanjing Road, Spectra + Partners' first project in Qingdao, is a 240-meter, 53-story mixed-use tower that integrates with the urban fabric while prioritizing sustainability with LEED V4 and WELL V2 certifications. The project features a pedestrian-friendly public realm with landscaped gardens, plazas, and retail spaces inspired by local rock formations.

The tower includes naturally ventilated offices, a luxurious three-story clubhouse with an infinity pool, and 105 high-end apartments offering panoramic sea views. It combines functionality and elegance, enhancing the city’s skyline and promoting a sustainable urban lifestyle.
